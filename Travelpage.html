Saint-Martin

Saint Martin's Island, located in the Bay of Bengal, is the only coral island in Bangladesh. Known for its clear blue waters, sandy beaches, and vibrant marine life, it's a popular tourist destination. The island's small size allows for easy exploration by foot or bicycle. It's also famous for its seafood, particularly fresh fish and lobsters. Additionally, the island is home to several species of sea turtles, making it an important ecological site.
